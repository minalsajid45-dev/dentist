Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to your questions about our dental services

Do you accept insurance?
Yes, we accept most major dental insurance providers. Please contact our office to verify your specific plan.
How often should I visit the dentist?
We generally recommend a checkup and cleaning every six months to maintain optimal oral health.
Do you offer emergency dental services?
Absolutely. If you have a dental emergency, please call our office immediately and we will do our best to see you the same day.
Are your treatments painful?
We use the latest technology and sedation options to ensure our treatments are as painless and comfortable as possible.
